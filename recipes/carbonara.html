Carbonara Recipe

Description

I fuckin' love lasagna

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • guancale
  • egg
  • parmesan cheese
  • pecorino romano cheese
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Render guancale slowly over low heat in frying pan
  2. Cook pasta (save the pasta water)
  3. Grate your cheese
  4. Separate egg white from yolk
  5. Whisk egg yolk with cheese and season with black pepeper
  6. Drain pasta, and immediately toss into pan. Turn off heat.
  7. Take pan off heat, stir in egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water as needed
